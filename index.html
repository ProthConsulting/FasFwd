Who We Are

Growth, Moving Forward

Fás Fwd is a consulting firm built for nonprofits and mission-driven organizations that are ready to move. Fás is the Irish word for growth. Fwd is where we are always headed. The name says it plainly: growth, moving forward.

We build the frameworks. You own the results. Every engagement leaves your organization more capable, not more dependent — with systems and strategies you can use Monday morning.

  • Deep sector expertise across fundraising, strategy, governance, and executive leadership
  • Practical frameworks built for accountability and real momentum
  • A philanthropic lens — we understand donor psychology and what moves people to give
  • Flexible models — project, fractional, or interim — sized to your needs and budget
